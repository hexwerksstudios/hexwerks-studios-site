What It Is

Shieldwall Country

Oaths & Banners focuses on gritty early medieval fights where formation, courage, leadership, and timing matter more than spectacle.

It is a game about keeping a line alive in mud and weather, where banners, leaders, and rough steel decide whether a warband advances as one body or frays at the edge.
