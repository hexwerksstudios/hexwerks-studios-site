What It Is

One Fight. No Waste.

EINVÍGI is built for fast setup and tense play, making a single duel feel like a complete story rather than a warm-up.

The prototype leans on compact fighter boards, clear action commitment, and a ring of stone that keeps every eye on the duel.

How It Plays

The Duel Loop

Each player commands one warrior inside the killing ground. The fight turns on distance, hidden intent, action commitment, and the nerve to read the opponent before iron answers.

Intent

Read Before You Strike

Players choose hidden intent, then reveal how their warriors commit to pressure, defense, movement, or violence.

Hazel Staves

The Ring Has Teeth

Hazel Staves define the sacred edge. Footwork can win space, threaten Ring-Out, or trap a fighter where wounds become likely.

Tempo

Wyrd and Momentum

Wyrd and Momentum push the duel between fate and initiative, rewarding timing without letting either warrior feel safe.

Cost

Overreach and Wounds

Action commitment can overextend a fighter. Overreach, wounds, and the ring edge make every bold move carry a price.
