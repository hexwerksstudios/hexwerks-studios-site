Black Oaths

Black Oaths is a grimdark Viking skirmish game of blood, faith, prophecy, and brutal close combat. Warriors do not fight for victory points alone. They fight for oaths, glory, doom, and the favor of powers older than kings.

EINVÍGI

EINVÍGI is a tense Norse dueling game built around movement, commitment, attack, defense, and violent follow-through. Every step matters. Every decision can become the moment your saga ends.

Miniatures & Lore

Hexwerks Studios creates characters, factions, and creatures shaped by old sagas, grim folklore, sacred violence, and the creeping shadow of faith.
