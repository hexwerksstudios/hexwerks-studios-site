Drengr or Níðingr

A Norse duel game of oaths, Wyrd, and sacred ground. Two warriors enter the hazel-bound ground. Kin watch from the edge. Oaths have been sworn. Words have failed. Iron will answer.

Each player commands one warrior in a compact ritual duel of hidden intent, Hazel Staves, Action Cards, Wyrd, Momentum, Overreach, Angle, wounds, and Ring-Out.

About Hexwerks Studios

Handmade Worlds for the Painted Table

Hexwerks Studios makes compact tabletop miniatures games rooted in grim folklore, historical myth, and physical hobby craft.

The games are built around named fighters, clear table states, sharp commitments, and moments that make a painted model feel like it has a history.
